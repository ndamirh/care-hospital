How to Make an Appointment

Online:

  • Visit our website and navigate to the "Appointments" section.
  • Fill out your information including name, contact, and preferred time.
  • Select a doctor or department.
  • Confirm your appointment and receive a confirmation via email or SMS.

In-Person:

  • Visit the hospital's reception desk.
  • Provide your details and the reason for your visit.
  • Select an available time slot.
  • Receive a printed appointment slip with details.
